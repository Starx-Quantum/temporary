Our Specialized Services

A dependable, compassionate presence you can trust.

Holmium LASER for Kidney

Holmium laser lithotripsy is a minimally invasive surgical procedure used to treat kidney stones. The laser is used to break down or fragment kidney stones into smaller pieces, making them easier to pass through the urinary tract.

24Hr/7 Days Support

Ensuring continuous care, our hospital is proud to offer 24/7 support, 365 days a year. Your well-being is our priority, and our dedicated team is here for you around the clock.

Urethral and Prostate Care

Specializing in Urethral and Prostate Care, our expert team provides comprehensive and personalized solutions for your urological health, ensuring comfort and well-being.

Hernia and Testicular Tumor Treatments

Specializing in hernia and testicular tumor treatments, our expert team provides comprehensive care, combining advanced medical interventions with personalized attention for optimal patient outcomes.

Laparoscopic Procedures

Experience advanced care with our laparoscopic procedures, providing minimally invasive solutions for faster recovery and improved patient outcomes.

Mini PCNL for Kidney Stones

Experience advanced kidney stone treatment with Mini PCNL – a minimally invasive procedure providing precision and swift recovery for optimal patient comfort and well-being.
